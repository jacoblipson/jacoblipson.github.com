  • Spring Rush, 2014

    Fraternity Rush begins on Monday, January 20th, and Theta Chi is looking forward to an exciting week of events. Freshmen, sophomores, and juniors are eligible to come join us for Skyzone trampoline dodgeball, Flatbread pizza, Redbones barbecue, and of course, a lot of opportunities to hang out with the brotherhood. Rush is non-binding, and anyone remotely interested in greek life, scholastic achievement, philanthropy, social events, or community leadership is encouraged to show up and get to know the brotherhood. Dates and times for rush events are as follows:

    Monday, January 20th at 7:00 PM: Flatbread pizza and bowling

    Wednesday, January 22nd at 6:00 PM: Trampoline Dodgeball at Skyzone

    Friday, January 24th at 6:00 PM: Redbones barbecue at the house

    All events meet at the Theta Chi house at 100 Packard Ave. All rushes should fill out the following forms: Tufts Fraternity Rush Registration & Theta Chi Rush Estimates. Additionally, if you plan on coming to Skyzone with us (and you should), you need to fill out this waiver: Skyzone Waiver. Any questions about rush events, the spring recruitment process, or Theta Chi at large should contact Jeff Aalberg at jeffrey_j.aalberg@tufts.edu or (207)-317-0109 (text messages welcome).
