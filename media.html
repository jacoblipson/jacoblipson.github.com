Produced by six high school students, feature-length documentary The Border explores illegal immigration and its many facets, from economics to justice to human rights. The film debuted at the Phoenix International Film Festival in May 2011 as a showcase feature, aired on CSPAN, and enjoyed a run in theaters in three different cities.
